************************************** Tetra Master *******************************************
***********************************************************************************************
**************************************** Objective ********************************************
***********************************************************************************************
*Flip your opponent's cards before they flip yours.                                           *
*To flip a card, the number on your card must be greater                                      *
*than the number that is on the adjacent side of your opponent's card                         *
***********************************************************************************************
*Example:    Your Card  Opponent Card                                                         *
*		            4	      4                                                                     *
*      	      4   8	  6   4                                                                   *
*	            	4	      4                                                                     *
***********************************************************************************************
*These two cards appear on the board side by side with your card to the left of your          *
*opponent's card.                                                                             *
*The 8 on your card is greater than the 6 on your opponent's card, therefore your opponent's  *
*card flips and you gain 1 point.                                                             * 
*The player with the most points at the end wins.                                             *
***********************************************************************************************
